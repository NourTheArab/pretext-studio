PretextStudio

A small studio for a paragraph

Give a paragraph a job.

A story can carry a setting. An explainer can make space for a moving idea. A reading exercise can keep pace without becoming another app. Pretext Studio lets you make one of those scenes, keep the text real, and take its configuration to a site you control.

No account. No upload. Just a scene you can keep.

First, three ways a paragraph can go somewhere.

A story can have a world around it.

Words leave the tap, ride the stream, and gather in the basin. Living text can be play before it is anything else.

Give an idea a setting.

Each element sets the passage, the palette, and the thing in motion. The fixed anchor shapes the text while the moving element follows its authored path.

Explain a journey as it moves.

Earth, the Moon, and Orion each get a place in the same note.
